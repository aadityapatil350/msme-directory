### 1. **Stand Up India Scheme** ✅
- **Status:** ACTIVE (Extended till 2025)
- **Corrected URL:** https://www.standupmitra.in/ (was .gov.in)
- **Updates:**
  - Confirmed scheme extended till 2025
  - Added accurate statistics (2.75 lakh+ beneficiaries)
  - Clarified margin money (25%) and credit guarantee details
- **Sources:**
  - [Official Stand-Up India Portal](https://www.standupmitra.in/)
  - [PIB Press Release](https://www.pib.gov.in/PressReleaseIframePage.aspx?PRID=1737320)

### 2. **PM Mudra Yojana - Tarun** ✅
- **Status:** ACTIVE with NEW LIMITS (Budget 2024)
- **Loan Amount:** **INCREASED from ₹10 lakh to ₹20 lakh**
- **New Category:** Tarun Plus (₹10L-₹20L) for repeat borrowers
- **Effective Date:** October 24, 2024
- **Sources:**
  - [Official Mudra Website](https://www.mudra.org.in/)
  - [PIB Announcement](https://www.pib.gov.in/PressReleaseIframePage.aspx?PRID=2069170)

### 3. **PM Vishwakarma Yojana** ✅
- **Status:** ACTIVE (2023-2028)
- **Verified URL:** https://pmvishwakarma.gov.in/
- **Clarified Loan Structure:**
  - Tranche 1: ₹1 lakh (18 months)
  - Tranche 2: ₹2 lakh (30 months)
  - Total: ₹3 lakh at 5% interest
- **Additional:** ₹15,000 toolkit grant + ₹500/day training stipend
- **Sources:**
  - [PM Vishwakarma Official](https://pmvishwakarma.gov.in/)
  - [IBEF Report](https://www.ibef.org/government-schemes/pm-vishwakarma-yojana)

### 4. **CGTMSE** ✅
- **Status:** ACTIVE with Dec 2024 enhancements
- **Loan Limit:** Up to ₹2 crore (was ₹5 crore in old data)
- **Guarantee Coverage:** 75-85% of loan amount
- **New:** Enhanced coverage for women-led enterprises (Dec 2024)
- **Effective From:** April 1, 2025
- **Sources:**
  - [CGTMSE Official](https://www.cgtmse.in/)
  - [Scheme Document April 2025](https://www.cgtmse.in/Default/ViewFile/?id=1743176302611)

### 5. **Startup India Seed Fund** ✅
- **Status:** ACTIVE (Accepts applications year-round)
- **Verified URL:** https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/
- **Funding:**
  - Grant: Up to ₹10 lakh (proof of concept)
  - Investment: Up to ₹50 lakh (market entry)
- **Outlay:** ₹945 crore over program period
- **Sources:**
  - [Startup India Seed Fund Portal](https://seedfund.startupindia.gov.in/)
  - [MyScheme.gov.in](https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/sisfs-fs)

### 6. **PMEGP** ✅
- **Status:** ACTIVE (Extended 2021-26)
- **Verified URL:** https://www.kviconline.gov.in/pmegpeportal/pmegphome/index.jsp
- **Extension:** Approved for 15th Finance Commission cycle (2021-22 to 2025-26)
- **Subsidy:** 15-35% of project cost
- **Sources:**
  - [KVIC PMEGP Portal](https://www.kviconline.gov.in/pmegpeportal/pmegphome/index.jsp)
  - [MSME Official](https://msme.gov.in/1-prime-ministers-employment-generation-programme-pmegp)

### 7. **PM SVANidhi** ✅
- **Status:** ACTIVE and EXTENDED till March 31, 2030
- **Major Update:** Restructured and extended beyond Dec 2024
- **Target:** 1.15 crore beneficiaries (50 lakh new)
- **Total Outlay:** ₹7,332 crore
- **Loan Tranches:** ₹10K → ₹20K → ₹50K
- **Helpline:** 1800 11 1979
- **Sources:**
  - [PM SVANidhi Official](https://pmsvanidhi.mohua.gov.in/)
  - [PIB Cabinet Approval](https://www.pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=2161157)

### 8. **Udyam Registration** ✅
- **Status:** ACTIVE (Over 2.46 crore registrations)
- **Verified URL:** https://udyamregistration.gov.in/
- **Key Stats:** 2.46+ crore MSMEs registered as of 2025
- **Features:** Free, lifetime validity, no renewal required
- **Sources:**
  - [Udyam Registration Portal](https://udyamregistration.gov.in/)
  - [FAQ Document](https://msmedigangtok.gov.in/assets/docs/faq/FAQ%20Udyam%20Registration%20dated%208%20July%202024.pdf)

- **Coverage:** 51 sub-sectors/products
- **Sources:**
  - [CLCSS Official](https://clcss.dcmsme.gov.in/)
